The information included has been verified by EPICORE, a world-wide network of public health professionals, ensuring a large spectrum of verification activities in proximity to where events are reported. EPICORE members give highest priority to reliable sources of information including documentation about events, official statements and complementary reliable reports obtained at local levels. EPICORE does not aim to replace any official reporting system and is meant to be a complementary surveillance tool supporting public health actors in their activities.
