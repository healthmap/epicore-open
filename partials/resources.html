Resources


We value your participation. These resources are available to you to thank you for being an EpiCore member. Resources that become available will be updated regularly and new opportunities highlighted in quarterly newsletters.

Center for Global Health Science and Security at Georgetown’s Event-Based Surveillance (EBS) Curriculum

This is a structured user-friendly EBS curriculum designed to prepare health officials at the national and sub-national levels to use formal and informal reports for early warning and response.
ghss.georgetown.edu/ebs

Tools

International Society for Infectious Diseases’ Guide to Infection Control in the Hospital, 5th Edition

This handy pocket-sized manual contains 60 chapters that explain key principles and guidelines for reducing the rate of nosocomial infections and practical measures intended to improve quality of care, minimize risk, save lives, and reduce costs. As an EpiCore member you are eligible for a free downloadable PDF version of this guide.
