How it Works

EpiCore links a worldwide member network of health professionals in order to provide verification of suspected or rumored disease outbreaks.

EpiCore requesters look for leads regarding possible public health events around the world using any source of information

When evidence of a public health event is found, EpiCore requesters will send a request for information (RFI) to the members of a specific geographic region in order to verify the information  

Through a secure online platform, members are able to easily and quickly provide (if preferred anonymously) details and local expertise which speeds the event verification or update  

The information collected through the platform is shared on the EpiCore Public RFI dashboard as a event summary as well as disseminated via other communication channels by each requester
