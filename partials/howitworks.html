How it Works

EpiCore links a worldwide member network of health professionals to verify information on disease outbreaks.

When evidence of outbreaks is found, local information is requested from EpiCore members. Through a secure online platform, members are able to easily and quickly provide local expertise that speeds outbreak verification. The information collected through EpiCore is then shared on ProMED-mail, which is a free, nonpolitical system that has an open and public worldwide distribution.
