The Founding Partners

Ending Pandemics

Ending Pandemics incubates, creates and supports innovative approaches for large scale impact across climate change, water security, pandemics, nuclear proliferation and Middle East conflict.

HealthMap

HealthMap, a freely-available website, brings together disparate online data sources to achieve a unified and comprehensive view of the current global state of infectious diseases and their effect on human and animal health.

ProMED-mail of the International Society for Infectious Diseases

Founded in 1994, ProMED-mail rapidly disseminates to its large global audience early warning reports on outbreaks of emerging and re-emerging diseases. Reports are selected and interpreted by a panel of specialists who provide expert commentary.

TEPHINET (The Training Programs in Epidemiology & Public Health Interventions Network)

Founded in 1997, TEPHINET is a member-led global network of field epidemiology training programs (FETPs) consisting of 65 field based FETPs in 90 countries and 5 regional training networks with over 17,500 trainees and graduates.
