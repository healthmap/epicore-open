About

EpiCore is a system that aims to complement existing surveillance methods and speed up the process of finding, reporting and verifying public health events.

EpiCore is linking a worldwide network of health experts that, through a secure online reporting platform, have the opportunity to provide information about ongoing public health events.

Epicore members are potentially contributing to a faster public health response of communities and health organisations involved at local and international levels.

EpiCore members have access to exclusive content related to infectious disease prevention and control, including onlinecourses (Webber training), online publications (selected articles from The Lancet Infectious Diseases) and scientific documentation (ISID guidelines).

The Founding Partners

Ending Pandemics

Ending Pandemics works with partners across the globe to unlock the potential to find, verify, and contain outbreaks faster no matter where they might emerge on the planet.

HealthMap

HealthMap, a freely-available website, brings together disparate online data sources to achieve a unified and comprehensive view of the current global state of infectious diseases and their effect on human and animal health.

ProMED-mail of the International Society for Infectious Diseases

Founded in 1994, ProMED-mail rapidly disseminates to its large global audience early warning reports on outbreaks of emerging and re-emerging diseases. Reports are selected and interpreted by a panel of specialists who provide expert commentary.

TEPHINET (The Training Programs in Epidemiology & Public Health Interventions Network)

Founded in 1997, TEPHINET is a member-led global network of field epidemiology training programs (FETPs) consisting of 65 field based FETPs in 90 countries and 5 regional training networks with over 17,500 trainees and graduates.
