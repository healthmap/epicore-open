What is Epicore?

EpiCore is a new system that aims to find and report outbreaks faster than traditional disease surveillance methods. It draws on the knowledge of a global network of health professionals, and connects it with a secure online networking and reporting platform. By using innovative surveillance techniques and linking local and international health experts, EpiCore enables faster outbreak detection and reporting. EpiCore is a partnership between the Skoll Global Threats Funds, HealthMap, Pro-MED Mail, and TEPHINET
