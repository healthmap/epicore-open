EpiCore Training

Welcome to EpiCore!

Congratulations on your acceptance! The next step is for you to finish short certification training sessions online.

These sessions are designed to provide the basic skills and information you need as a health professional to improve disease surveillance not only in your community and country, but also globally.

The journey to EpiCore certification begins by clicking the button below, where you will be directed to watch a 20xx-minute video titled "Introduction to Innovative Disease Surveillance Training Course". This video will provide you with the ability to:

  • Recognize the advantages and challenges of traditional disease surveillance
  • Recognize informal information sources
  • Define the meaning of innovative disease surveillance
  • Identify the benefits of using innovative disease surveillance methods

Once done, you can click on "Proceed to the second Video" button, where you will be directed to watch a xx-minute video titled “The EpiCore Training Course” which will give you all the information you need to use the EpiCore platform efficiently.

Finally, you will be directed to a final page to take the short test that is required for certification. Once you have passed the test, you will gain all the privileges of a full EpiCore member where you will have access to your EpiCore dashboard and will begin receiving RFIs on outbreaks in your region.

We are very excited to start working with you soon! And please do not hesitate to contact us at info@epicore.org if you have any questions.

Sincerely,
-The EpiCore Team
