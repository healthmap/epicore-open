Welcome to EpiCore!

Congratulations on your acceptance! The next step is for you to complete our short online training certification sessions.

These sessions are designed to provide the basic skills and information you need as a health professional to improve disease surveillance not only in your community and country, but also globally.

The journey to EpiCore certification begins by clicking the button below, where you will be directed to the first session consisting of a 20 minute video titled "Introduction to Innovative Disease Surveillance Training Course". Please watch this video and learn the following:

  • Recognize the advantages and challenges of traditional disease surveillance
  • Define the meaning of innovative disease surveillance
  • Recognize informal information sources
  • Identify the benefits of using innovative disease surveillance methods

Above this video you will find a link where you can download the Introduction to Innovative Diseases Surveillance Training Course Handbook.

Next, proceed to the second session where you will need to download and read the EpiCore Course Training Course handbook. The handbook will go over the following:

  • What is EpiCore and how ProMED is related to EpiCore
  • Why you should become an EpiCore member and what you will gain from it
  • What a request for Information is and how to respond
  • How to use the EpiCore platform

After you finish viewing the videos or handbooks, you will be directed to take a short test. Once you pass the test, you will be a certified EpiCore member. You will have access to your EpiCore dashboard and will begin receiving Requests for Information on possible outbreaks in your region.

We are very excited to start working with you! Please do not hesitate to contact us at info@epicore.org if you have any questions.

Sincerely,
The EpiCore Team
